What to expect

Your appointment begins with custom mapping to honour your facial architecture followed by gentle, feather-light strokes that mimic real hair. Pigments are blended to harmonise with your complexion for a seamless, lived-in finish.

Consultation & mapping

We define the perfect outline with VisionOS-inspired projection tools and agree on your desired density before any pigment is placed.

Feathered application

Ultra-fine strokes are layered in gentle passes, building natural fullness while maintaining a soft gradient at the front of the brow.

Finishing ritual

We seal brows with cooling gel and capture after photos in a glass-lit setting so you leave with the ultimate reveal.

Aftercare essentials

  • Keep brows dry for 48 hours to lock in pigment.
  • Apply the provided barrier ointment morning and night for 7 days.
  • Avoid saunas, heavy workouts, and retinoids during the first two weeks.
  • Schedule your complimentary touch-up 6–8 weeks post appointment.
